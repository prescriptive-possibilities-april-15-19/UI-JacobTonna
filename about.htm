We are awesome

To put it simply we are awesome! We all started as students at Lambda School in 2019, we learned the skills necessary to proudly call ourselves software engineers. During the month of April, 2019 we all decided to join Michael Beck in designing this open source, universal tool to better the lives of millions at 0 cost to them. If that doesn't make us modern-day super hero's, I don't know what would.

All jokes aside, each and every person on this team has contributed a significant amount of their time to carefully curate this life changing tool. It's our hope that someday history books will speak of us and this project as Ambitious & Successful in its goals.

Free Forever, we will never ask anyone to pay for this public service before or after use.
